Half Girlfriend is an Indianyoung adult romance >novel by Indian author Chetan Bhagat .The novel, set in rural Bihar, New Delhi, Patna, and New York, is the story of a Bihari on a quest of winning over the girl he loves . This is Bhagat's sixth novel, which was released on 1 October 2014 by Rupa Publications .The novel has also been published in Hindi and Gujarati.
